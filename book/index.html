What's it about?

The book I'm writing is about me. I started writing it to give my friends and family a glimpse at my inner world, and to record the stories from my life that I don't ever want to forget. But I'm also happy to share it with anyone who's interested in the story of a kid who grew up in the middle of nowhere and then took a bit of an unusual path through life.


When will it be done?

This first volume is written. It just needs a bit more editing to get it ready to publish. If you would like to show your support and join me on that journey, check out my Patreon where I'll be posting the chapters as I polish them up, as well as blogging a bit about the process of self-publishing a "novelette".
