Scheduler App

I was contracted to develop a scheduler application for LaneLight Traffic Technologies. It is embedded in their development website and is compatible with Chrome, Firefox, and Edge. The app consists of a calender and various settings used to setup and test in-road pedestrian crosswalk lights.

Train Control and Maintenance UIs

For three years I was the lead front-end developer at Quester Tangent for their on-train monitoring and diagnositics systems. I created the front-end framework for the built-in crew displays and maintainer/engineer portable display units (aka laptops). This includes both the graphical user interfaces as well as the web servers that exchange data with each of the train's subsystems. See my resume for more details.
