I:\ Volume 1: Origin Story

I wrote a book!

About four years ago--after graduating from the Computer and Electronics Engineering Technology program at Camosun College--I suddenly found myself with an abundance of free time in my evenings and weekends. So I started working towards my life-long goal of writing a book.

My original plan was to write a longer piece that followed a more traditional format, but last year I decided to break it down into a series of shorter reads instead. Thanks to the support of my patrons, I was able to hire an editor and prepare a polished and professional book that I'm incredibly proud of.

This first volume consists of the stories from my early childhood that I've told a thousand times throughout my life, making them the perfect place to start. They are the foundation of my experience and go a surprisingly long way towards answering that perennial question we each must answer for ourselves:

Who am I?
